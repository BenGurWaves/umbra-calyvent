UMBRA Privacy / Rev. 2026

Privacy

umbra.calyvent.com · 100% client-side

UMBRA runs entirely inside your browser. Your shadow parameters are never uploaded, transmitted, or stored on any server. There is nothing for us to see.

01 · No upload, no server

Every operation — calculating multi-layered ambient shadows using power-coefficient interpolation, generating CSS box-shadow properties, mapping to Tailwind arbitrary variants, and rendering the live preview — is performed locally using JavaScript executing in your browser. Your inputs do not leave your device.

02 · What we store

UMBRA uses your browser's local storage to keep a short history of your recent shadow configurations so you can restore them within a session. This data lives only on your device, is never sent anywhere, and can be erased at any time by clearing your browser storage.

03 · No accounts, no cookies, no tracking

There are no user accounts, no authentication, and no tracking cookies. We do not run third-party advertising or behavioural analytics on the tool. We cannot identify you.

04 · Third-party code

The application loads only its own first-party scripts. No external code executes against the contents of your inputs.

05 · Children

UMBRA is a general-purpose developer utility and is not directed at children under 13.

06 · Changes

If this notice changes, the revised version will be posted here. Because the tool stores nothing about you, changes only affect how the utility behaves on your device going forward.

07 · Contact

UMBRA is built and maintained by Velocity, a Calyvent studio. Questions may be directed there.
